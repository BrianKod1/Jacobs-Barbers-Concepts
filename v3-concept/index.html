About
"Still cutting. Still listening."

Lucyna Jacobs started cutting hair at five years old — dolls first, then family and friends brave enough to sit still. What began as a habit became a trade: she trained and certified through the Ontario College of Trades and the Ministry of Training, Colleges, and Universities.

The shop first opened on The Queensway in October 2016. When the pandemic forced a shutdown in March 2020 and the building was later sold, Lucyna relocated and reopened at 170 North Queen Street in March 2022 — where the same loyal customers found her again, alongside plenty of new ones.

That same care shapes every appointment today — a small, personal barbering practice built one client at a time.
